About

Local expertise. Real community.

Blue Ox Running supports runners of all levels in the Chippewa Valley—helping people find the right products, stay healthy, and build community through events and weekly runs.

Mission

  • Help runners find the right footwear and gear
  • Support healthy training habits and injury prevention
  • Build an inclusive community for all experience levels
