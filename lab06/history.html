How It All Started

The first Tour de France was held in 1903 as a way to promote the newspaper L'Auto. It quickly grew into the most prestigious race in professional cycling.

Over more than a century, the race has survived wars, scandals, and rule changes, but the basic idea remains the same: three weeks of intense racing across France.

Famous Jerseys

  • Yellow Jersey (Maillot Jaune): Overall race leader.
  • Green Jersey: Points leader (sprinters).
  • Polka Dot Jersey: Best climber (King of the Mountains).
  • White Jersey: Best young rider (under 26).
