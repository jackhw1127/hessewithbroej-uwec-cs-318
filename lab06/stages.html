Mountain Stages

Iconic climbs in the Alps and Pyrenees, such as Alpe d'Huez and the Col du Tourmalet, often decide the overall winner.

  1. Alpe d'Huez – the legendary 21 hairpins.
  2. Mont Ventoux – windswept and brutal.
  3. Col du Tourmalet – a classic Pyrenean climb.

Final Stage in Paris

The race traditionally finishes on the Champs-Élysées in Paris. Sprinters fight for glory while the overall leader celebrates with their team.
